About Me

My background

I have a strong interest in web design and digital creativity. To me, this kind of work brings together both technical skills and aesthetics. A website feels like a vibrant product. I'm also drawn to the business side of it, which involves a lot of creativity and potential. Learning the skills and knowledge in this field will help me take part in this exciting space.

My interests

One of my favorite hobbies is finding great food spots. Whether it’s a street vendor selling skewers or a hidden noodle shop, I love exploring new flavors and the stories behind them. Food has deep meaning in culture and is also one of life’s great pleasures.
